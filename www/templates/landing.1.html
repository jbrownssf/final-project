Welcome to Simply Scheduling!
  • - This app is designed for either seasonal or small companies.
  • - It helps keep track of any changes made to the published schedules so that you, as an employer, can see who changed the schedule and when.
  • - When a user views a schedule they are a part of, it tells them where and when they are working.
  • - It also makes a note so that a manager or employer can see if and when a user saw their assigned schedule.
